Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between Trezor Model T and Trezor One?

The Trezor Model T offers a touchscreen and advanced security features, while the Trezor One is a more affordable option with basic security features.

How do I set up my Trezor device?

To set up your Trezor device, follow the instructions provided in the user manual or visit our website for a step-by-step guide.

Is my Trezor device compatible with all cryptocurrencies?

Trezor devices support a wide range of cryptocurrencies. Please check our website for a full list of supported coins and tokens.

How do I update the firmware on my Trezor device?

To update the firmware on your Trezor device, connect it to your computer and follow the instructions provided in the Trezor Suite application.

Where can I find more information about Trezor products?

For more information about Trezor products, visit our website or contact our customer support team.
